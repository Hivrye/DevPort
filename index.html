A Professional Workflow in 3 Simple Steps

1. Connect Your Repository

Link a DevPort project to your existing GitHub repo. No need to change your development workflow. You live in your code editor and GitHub; we handle the rest.

2. Invite Your Client to Their Portal

Send your client a private, professional link to their project dashboard. They can see high-level progress, submit new requests, and track the status of everything in one place.

3. Watch the Magic Happen

Client requests are automatically converted into new, detailed issues in your GitHub repo. When you close an issue in GitHub, the status updates for the client. Zero manual work required.
